| **Photographer** | Count the number of birds you have played that have one of the following colors in their name: ash, black, blue, bronze, brown, cerulean, chestnut, ferruginous, gold, gray, green, indigo, lazuli, purple, red, rose, roseate, ruby, ruddy, rufous, snowy, white, yellow. ## Nest/Egg-Related Bonuses

| Bonus Card Name | Scoring Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Wildlife Gardener, Platform Builder, Enclosure Builder, Nest Box Builder** | Count the number of birds you played that have the specific nest symbol shown on the card. Star nests are wild and count toward all four of these bonuses. |
| **Breeding Manager** | Count the number of birds that have at least 4 egg tokens on them at the end of the game. |
| **Oologist** | Count only bird cards that have at least 1 egg token on them at the end of the game. |

## Habitat-Related Bonuses

| Bonus Card Name | Scoring Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Forester, Prairie Manager, Wetland Scientist** | Count the number of birds you played that can only live in the one specific habitat shown on this bonus card. If a bird has the option to live in more than one type of habitat, it cannot count toward this bonus. |
| **Ecologist** | Count the number of birds you played in the habitat where you have the fewest birds. If you have two habitats tied for the fewest birds, you may still score this bonus. For example, if all of your habitats have 3 birds in them, your habitat with the fewest birds has 3 birds in it. |

## Other Bonuses

| Bonus Card Name | Scoring Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Bird Counter, Falconer** | Count the birds you played that have the specific symbol shown on this bonus card. The symbol is on the left-hand side of the bird’s brown power. |
| **Large Bird Specialist** | Count the birds you played that have wingspans over 65cm. Wingspans of exactly 65cm do not count toward this bonus. |
| **Passerine Specialist** | Count the birds you played that have wingspans 30cm or less. Wingspans of exactly 30cm count toward this bonus. |
| **Backyard Birder** | Count the birds you played that have a face value of 0, 1, 2, or 3 points. |
| **Visionary Leader** | Count the bird cards still in your hand at the end of the game. |
